IMAGE FOLDER - Pet Photos
===========================

Place your pet photos here with these names:
- pet1.jpg
- pet2.jpg
- pet3.jpg
- pet4.jpg
- pet5.jpg
- pet6.jpg

Recommended specifications:
- Size: 800x800px or larger
- Format: JPG, JPEG, or PNG
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1) works best

The website will display these images in the gallery section with beautiful hover effects.

If you don't have photos yet, you can download free stock pet photos from:
- Unsplash.com
- Pexels.com
- Pixabay.com

Simply save them with the correct filenames (pet1.jpg, pet2.jpg, etc.) in this folder.
